This is the projects page, here you will find many projects from grade levels I've gone to, which right now is 8th grade. Some of the projects of my grade are the skin color project, and the skin cancer calendar project. At DRSS we do project base learning, which is where we learn through projects themselves. Project base learning is very important because it helps me learn and grow faster then normal. Without it I probally wouldn't be as smart, and thanks to it I became more creative and persistent. Collaboration is a big part of Project based learning because most of the projects we do have something to do with collaboration, whether or not it's critique or working with a partner we always have at least a little bit of collaboration.
