Coding Progression

On this page I will describe what I grew the most in this year, and all the tags or terms I learned through coding. I grew the most in the div element this year, the div element is a tag that spaces text. It may not seem that useful, but if you use it right it can make a website look 10 times better. At first I just used it to center everthing, but as I learned it more I started to use it to move things around. Below is an example of me moving things around, and pictures of another example on one of my other pages. The picture on the left shows my code, and the placement of div elements, the picture on the right shows what it looks like inside the webpage.
